GRIP is a privacy-first digital discipline app for college students that helps them stay focused without feeling judged. Instead of boring reminders, it motivates with campus-relevant, relatable nudges like “Library time > reel time” or “Future placement loading…”. Its core feature, CLUTCH Mode, lets users lock in during exams, deadlines, or late-night study sessions using aggregate, privacy-safe screen-time insights, without tracking app content, messages, or personal data. GRIP reframes productivity as quiet discipline and self-respect, making focus feel cool, socially acceptable, and easy to adopt even for students who usually avoid traditional productivity tools.
